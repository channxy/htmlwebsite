About Janja Garnbret

Janja Garnbret is a Slovenian professional climber known for her dominance in sport climbing. She won the first-ever gold medal in Sport Climbing at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics.

Olympic & World Cup Career

Tokyo 2020 Olympics

Gold Medalist in Sport Climbing.

IFSC World Cup

Multiple-time winner in Bouldering and Lead Climbing.

Clean Sweep 2019

First climber to win every Bouldering World Cup event in a single season.
